The Courage to Doubt

The situation of being a theologian in an AI-Lab has its own charm... and its own difficulties. I use to describe this situation with the expression "The Courage to Doubt" - an expression I have chosen in accordance with the central expression in the theology of Paul Tillich: The Courage to Be. An explanation of this expression can be found in a talk in November 1995 at Harvard Divinity School: The Courage to Doubt: How to Build Android Robots as a Theologian
