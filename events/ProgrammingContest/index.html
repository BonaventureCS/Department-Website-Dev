Announcing the Results of

High School Programming Contest

Friday, March 22, 2013

Winners of the 2013 Competition:

The Dalton School, New York, New York

The Dalton School of New York, NY, won the 25th annual high school programming contest hosted by St. Bonaventure University's Computer Science Department on Friday, March 22.  Ten high schools brought twelve teams of up to four students per team.  The contest was held on the St. Bonaventure University campus.
