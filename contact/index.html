The St. Bonaventure campus is situated between the village of Allegany and the city of Olean on Route 417, which is an extension of West State Street from the city of Olean. The main campus entrance is at the intersection of Route 417 and Constitution Avenue, directly across from Country Inn & Suites. The entrance is about two miles from exits 24 or 25 of Interstate 86. Upon entering campus, there is a vehicle pull-off spot and a lighted map to direct you.
