I am a member of the Computer Science Department, which I joined in 1987. I was chair of the Department from 1988 - 2003. I hold the rank of Professor of Computer Science. My areas of specialization are database management systems, Windows and Web application programming, and robotics. I have developed several database and web-based applications for local area businesses with undergraduate students and have worked with students to develop the courseware we use in the artificial intelligence and robotics courses.
